About Us

Welcome to Lelei Travel Agency

Welcome to Lelei Travel Agency, your trusted partner in overseas employment opportunities. We are a Recruitment Agency that is focused in helping our clients to land in their dream jobs that are well paid. With offices in Eldoret and Nairobi, we are a fully registered company dedicated to helping individuals achieve their dream of working abroad. We specialize in facilitating job placements in countries such as Dubai, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia.

Our agency supports candidates seeking roles as riders, cleaners, security personnel, warehouse workers, and many more. At Lelei Travel Agency, we guide you through every step of the process with professionalism, transparency, and care, ensuring a smooth and reliable journey toward your international career goals.
